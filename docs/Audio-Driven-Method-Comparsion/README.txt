This folder contains comparison videos of our method and other methods. The pronunciation demonstrated is the same as in Figure 4, to illustrate the advantages of our algorithm for non-Indo-European languages.

JPG files with the "_portrait" suffix are single portraits that need to be animated (sourced from: https://thispersondoesnotexist.com/)

MP4 files with the "_video" suffix are the algorithm comparison results. From left to right, they show: SadTalker, EAT, PD-FGC, AniTalker, EDTalker, EchoMimic, VQTalker (Ours)

All video are resized to a unified resolution: 256*256.
